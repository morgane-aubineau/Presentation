Presentation

Welcome on my personal academic website! I am Morgane Aubineau, PhD in neurodevelopmental psychopathology. I am an avid learner, passionate about brain research and learning.
In this website, I provide information and resources about my current work on 1- learning and inclusion of teeangers with high functioning autism spectrum conditions and 2- the neural architecture of high cognitive ability (g) individuals. I also added some of the material I used to teach neuroscience to various audiences (from university students to gifted children). A link to visit the website of the Pearl lab will be available soon (currently under construction).

Keywords: brain imaging research; high cognitive ability (g); neurodevelopmental conditions (ASC,ADHD,giftedness); learning motivation; cognitive enhancement; polymathy.
